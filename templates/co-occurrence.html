Correlations Between Ingredients and Symptoms
The plot below shows the correlations between ingredients and symptoms in adverse event reports. Blue indicates positive correlations, while pink indicates negative correlations, and the intensity of the color indicates the magnitude of the correlation. Data are only shown for the ingredients and symptoms that occur most often in adverse event reports, and the axes are ordered by frequency of occurrence.
