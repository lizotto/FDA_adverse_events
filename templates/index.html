Understanding Cosmetic Adverse Events

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) oversees the multi-billion-dollar-per-year cosmetics industry, but it does not regulate products or ingredients before they are sold. This site uses the FDA's database of cosmetic adverse events combined with ingredient information to help consumers understand the links between products, their ingredients, and reported adverse events.
